ABOUT ME

My name is Steven Graf. I am currently living in McLouth, KS working as an Implementation Engineer for 911 Datamaster, Inc. I grew up in North Central Kansas and attended Fort Hays State University where I received my bachelors degree in Geography. Over the past few years I have developed a wide range of skills in the GIS and 911 industries including ArcGIS Enterprise, Model Builder, Python, Microsoft SQL Server and many others. I have always had a passion for technology and a strong interest in Geography. I'm always interested in the next opportunity that presents itself and to help a company grow.
